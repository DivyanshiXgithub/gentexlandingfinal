Fodder Bajra Dairy Plus

Multi-cut hybrid with quick regrowth, high biomass and excellent palatability for dairy animals.

Dairy Plus is a high biomass hybrid bajra specially bred for green fodder. It offers quick regrowth after each cut, soft juicy stems and high leaf–stem ratio, making it a reliable choice for dairy farmers.

Multi cut High protein Ideal for dairy farms
Key traits
  • First cut in about 45–50 days after sowing under good management.
  • Subsequent cuts possible every 25–30 days with proper nutrition and irrigation.
  • Tall plants with broad leaves and soft stem – animals accept fodder easily.
  • Good standability and tolerance to common foliar diseases.
Basic agronomy (general guideline)
  • Sowing rate: 4–5 kg seed/acre.
  • Spacing: line sowing at 1.5 ft between rows; thinner sowing for dense fodder stand.
  • Fertiliser: good response to FYM + nitrogen top dressings after each cut.
  • Irrigation: maintain adequate moisture for faster regrowth after cutting.
