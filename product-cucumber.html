Hybrid Cucumber Kayon F1

Early, vigorous hybrid with dark green uniform fruits – suitable for open field and protected cultivation.

Key features
  • Early maturity with strong plant vigour and continuous bearing.
  • Uniform cylindrical fruits, dark green colour and good length.
  • Suitable for summer and kharif sowing in most cucumber growing areas.
  • Good keeping quality and transportability for long-distance markets.
Recommended sowing guidelines
  • Sowing time: As per local climate – mainly Feb–Mar and Jun–Jul.
  • Spacing: 4–5 ft between rows, 1.5–2 ft between plants.
  • Seed rate: Approx. 400–500 g per acre.
  • Special notes: Use drip + mulching for better fruit size and yield.
Ideal for
  • Farmers supplying to nearby mandis and supermarket chains.
  • Growers using net house / low tunnel for off-season production.
  • Traders needing uniform lot and attractive fruit colour.
