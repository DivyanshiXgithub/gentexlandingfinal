Fodder management tips

To get maximum benefit from fodder crops:

  • Follow correct seed rate and maintain dense but manageable plant stand.
  • Cut at the right stage (boot to early flowering) for best nutrition.
  • After each cut, apply light irrigation and nitrogen top dressing for quick regrowth.
  • Use part of the biomass for silage to ensure year-round fodder availability.
