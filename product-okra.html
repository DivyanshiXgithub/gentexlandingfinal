Okra Hybrid Sunshine 101

Dark green tender pods with good keeping quality and tolerance to major okra diseases.

Sunshine 101 is a strong, high-yielding hybrid okra suitable for fresh market supply. Plants have good branching, heavy bearing and pods stay tender for a longer picking window.

Strong plants Regular picking Farmer preferred
Key traits
  • Dark green, attractive pods with uniform shape and length.
  • Early to first picking with good continuity of harvest.
  • Tolerance to major okra diseases and YVMV (field tolerance, not immunity).
  • Suitable for open field cultivation in most okra growing belts.
Basic agronomy (general guideline)
  • Sowing: 3–4 kg seed/acre depending on spacing and soil.
  • Spacing: 1.5–2 ft between rows, 0.6–1 ft between plants.
  • Picking: start 45–50 days after sowing; pick every 2–3 days.
