General agronomy tips

For best results with field crops:

  • Follow recommended sowing window for your agro-climatic zone.
  • Use seed treatment and balanced fertiliser doses based on soil test reports.
  • Maintain optimum plant population by using correct seed rate and spacing.
  • Plan timely weed and pest management to protect early crop growth.
